## Notes

- **D** = Day shift (0600–1400)
- **S** = Swing shift (1400–2200)
- **N** = Night shift (2200–0600)
- Each employee works 5 days on / 2 days off per week (consecutive days off)
- Days off are staggered across the week to maintain 24/7 coverage
- This schedule assigns employees to fixed shifts; to rotate shifts (e.g., monthly), swap shift assignments between groups
